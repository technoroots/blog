About Us

We are a small team of passionate minds who have been working in IT sector for around 8 years. We are technology enthusiasts having deep interest in the area of Unix ,Cloud Computing,Infra as Code, Python development, Machine Learning , Artificial intelligence and Data Science.

Starting as a fresher in IT sector and worked on projects related to finance, health care, banking, telecom etc over the years,we came up together with an idea to build a platform where the IT professionals and students can come to learn, grow and excel in their careers.

Our intention is to always start from the basic concepts and deep dive further into details of every subject that we cover inour blogs.We have tried our level best to keep the matter very simple and easy to understand.

In our free time, we like to hangout together ,go for hiking and photography,watch movies or documentaries and occasionally grab a drink or two together.
